About Us

Hi, we're a couple of Stanford Hemepath fellows who know that learning to diff bone marrows can be hard, but we also think it should be fun! That's why we made this game. Please feel free to contact us with any suggestions to improve the game, or other tutorials that you'd enjoy. You can find the source code and images on Github.
